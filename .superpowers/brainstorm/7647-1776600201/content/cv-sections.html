Which sections should your CV include?

Select all that apply — you can always add more later

A

Personal Info

Name, email, phone, LinkedIn, GitHub, location

B

Summary / Objective

A short professional bio or career objective paragraph

C

Work Experience

Job titles, companies, dates, bullet-point achievements

D

Education

Degrees, institutions, graduation years, GPA

E

Skills

Technical skills, languages, tools — grouped by category

F

Projects

Personal or open-source projects with links and descriptions

G

Certifications / Awards

Professional certifications, honors, recognitions

H

Publications / Research

Academic papers, blog posts, talks

I

Languages

Spoken languages with proficiency levels
